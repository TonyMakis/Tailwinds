We've Got Reads

Our aim is to deliver grade-A reading content

For years experts and people in general have both highlighted the benefits of reading. Reading between the lines, it seems that most people agree that reading expands your mental faculties, and your ability to understand concepts that are beyond superficial thinking.

“A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies . . . The man who never reads lives only one” – George R.R. Martin

It is our hope that by making profound reading material more accessible we will provide an avenue to mental stimulation that supercedes that which we experience most regularly in our day-to-day.

Usage

In the package.json in this project I have specified a node script (npm run build-css) to build CSS using tailwind. In the src directory, there is a styles.css file that implements any extra utilities used in the CSS of the project; after running the above script, a final styles.css file is generated in the public directory.

My favorite effect of this is that the amount of exposed CSS that we directly tweak is minimized.
